About 3 PILLARS

Leading Environmental & Cleanliness Partner for Smart Spaces

3 PILLARS is an environmentally focused cleanliness and waste collection company serving schools, colleges, hotels, hostels, and other commercial properties through professional contractual operations.

The organization is committed to maintaining hygienic, clean, and sustainable environments for students, guests, residents, and staff. The company operates with modern cleanliness standards, trained workforce management, and eco-conscious operational practices.

Our Vision

“Empowering cleaner environments with smart, sustainable, and future-ready waste management and facility solutions.”

Our Operational Standards

Ensuring safety, reliability, and ecological compliance across high-traffic environments.

Background Verified Staff

Every employee goes through strict criminal record verifications before property deployment.

100% Eco-Safe Sanitizers

We use non-toxic, eco-friendly, chemical-free sanitizers safe for guests, residents, and students.

Zero-Waste Landfill Initiatives

Segregating organic and recyclable materials at source to ensure minimal landfill impacts.

ISO Standard Quality Audits

Weekly hygiene scoring and routine random checks by field supervisors to maintain cleanliness.
